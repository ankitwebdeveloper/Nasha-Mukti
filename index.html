About Our Nasha Mukti Kendra

Dedicated to recovery, healing, and a healthier life for every individual.

Caring Support. Trusted Recovery..

Rohtak Nasha Mukti Kendra is a trusted and well-recommended rehabilitation center in Rohtak . We are dedicated to helping people break free from addiction and rebuild their lives with dignity and confidence. Our unique treatment approach focuses on motivating and healing patients in a way that they naturally start avoiding bad habits. We have 24×7 nursing staff and experienced doctors available to provide continuous care. We follow a fully scientific and humane treatment process — no torture, only proper medical and emotional support. With a high success rate, we proudly help most of our patients overcome addiction. In simple words, we are one of the most affordable and comfortable Nasha Mukti Kendras in Rohtak.
